Change Password

Click on “Settings”.

On the right panel, click on “Edit Settings”.

Under ‘Password”, tick the checkbox to change password.

Under the ‘Old Password’ field, enter in the old password provided to you via email.

Under the ‘New Password’ field, enter in your desired password.

Under the ‘Confirm Password’ field, re-enter in the same password as entered in the “New Password” field.

Click on “Update Settings” to proceed.

To access back to your course, click on the desired course. In this example, we will be using “SDE102- Developing Academic Writing Skills”.
